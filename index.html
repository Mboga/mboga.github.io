Nicholus Mboga

I work as Consultant in AI, Computer Vision and Data Engineering. An overview of my research output while working in Academia. I am interested in computer vision, deep learning and remote sensing. I have explored several applications using remote sensing imagery.

I obtained my Bachelor in Geomatics Engineering from Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology, Master's degree from University of Twente, and Doctor of Science from Université Libre de Bruxelles.

I did a post-doc in Machine Learning for Computer Vision and Signal Processing at INVILAB,the University of Antwerp.

My Research Interests are in Computer Vision, Deep Learning and Remote Sensing.
